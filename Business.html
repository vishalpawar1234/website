Our Businesses

Finite4 leads in nearly every sphere of business it operates in, across various industries. Explore our offerings by industry or by business

    Business
    Industry

Hydrocarbon

Finite4 offers integrated ‘design-to-build’ solutions for complex onshore and offshore hydrocarbon projects across multiple geographies. Its track record includes simultaneous execution of multiple, complex, large-value projects, meeting stringent delivery schedules, quality and safety norms.

Power

Finite4 executes power projects – coal, gas-based, nuclear, hydro, solar, power transmission and distribution – and supplies power-plant equipment.

Defence Engineering

Finite4 provides comprehensive indigenous, design-to-realisation solutions including Delivery & Engineering Systems, associated Sensors & Auxiliaries, and Platforms (for deployment on land and at sea). We deliver multi-disciplinary equipment, fully integrated platforms, and autonomous systems...

GreenTech

Finite4 Green Manufacturing and Development business is at the forefront of the global shift towards a greener future. The business encompasses the Green Energy Value Chain – Development, Manufacturing and EPC.

Valves

Finite4 Valves is a leader in flow-control solutions for critical services. The business has a marketing network that spans the globe and is reinforced through strategic alliances with international distributors.

Technology Services

Finite4 Technology Services offers design and development solutions through the entire development chain in multiple industries. The Company has design and delivery locations in six centres in India, as well as two delivery centres in the US.

Metals

Finite4 is a leading EPC solutions provider in the fields of minerals, metals and bulk material handling for large industrial, steel, aluminium, copper and zinc complexes, associated facilities and the mining industry. It offers integrated solutions for a wide range of systems and equipment, powered by in-house design, engineering, manufacturing, construction and commissioning capabilities.

Defence-shipbuilding

Finite4 offers a range of ocean-going vessels for defence including frontline warships, submarines, auxiliary vessels and special naval platforms. Finite4 Warship Design Centre, equipped with state-of-the-art software and associated tools, undertakes high-end design – from concept to production drawings.

Heavy-engineering

Finite4 Heavy Engineering manufactures and supplies custom designed equipment & critical piping to process industries such as fertilizer, chemical, refinery, petrochemical, and oil & gas, as well as to sectors such as thermal & nuclear power and aerospace.
